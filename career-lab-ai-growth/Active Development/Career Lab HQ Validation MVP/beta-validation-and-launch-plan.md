# Beta Validation And MVP Launch Plan

## Recruitment Plan

Target: 10-25 beta users.

Primary beta segments:

- Professionals seeking promotion in the next 3-12 months.
- Senior individual contributors considering leadership.
- First-time managers in their first year.
- Team leads without formal manager training.

Recruitment channels:

- Founder network.
- LinkedIn posts.
- Direct outreach to professionals with promotion or manager keywords.
- Alumni groups.
- HR and manager communities.
- Referrals from first beta users.

Screening criteria:

- Has a real career goal.
- Feels some urgency.
- Will complete assessment and weekly check-in.
- Is willing to pay at least $49-$99.

## Interview Guide

Before purchase:

1. What career outcome are you trying to create?
2. What have you tried already?
3. Why does this matter now?
4. What would make this worth paying for?
5. What would make you choose this over ChatGPT?

After report:

1. What part of the scorecard felt most accurate?
2. What surprised you?
3. What felt generic or not useful?
4. What action are you most likely to take?
5. Would you pay to continue weekly tracking?

After first weekly check-in:

1. Did the system help you act?
2. Was the next action clear?
3. What blocked completion?
4. Did you want human input?
5. Would you recommend this to someone in a similar situation?

## Success Criteria

Commercial:

- 40% of qualified beta users pay.
- 20% choose $299+ tier.
- 30% ask about continuing after first report.

Engagement:

- 80% complete assessment.
- 70% read/open report.
- 60% complete week 1 check-in.
- 40% complete week 4 check-in.

Outcome:

- 70% report improved clarity.
- 60% complete at least one career action.
- 50% capture at least one evidence item.
- 30% improve a score component within 30 days.

## Failure Criteria

Stop or change offer if:

- Users will not pay for diagnostic.
- Users say ChatGPT is enough.
- Users do not complete check-ins.
- Users cannot explain the value in one sentence.
- Users only want resume services.

## Metrics

Primary:

- Weekly Completed Career Actions Per Active Member.

Secondary:

- Paid assessment conversion.
- Assessment completion.
- Blueprint completion.
- Week 1 check-in completion.
- Score movement.
- Human review requests.
- Retention intent.
- Referrals.

## Human Escalation Framework

AI should handle 80-95% of interactions.

Escalate to human review when:

- Promotion score is 70+ and user is preparing a real conversation.
- User has salary negotiation within 30 days.
- User reports performance concern, PIP risk, or serious manager conflict.
- User has repeated blocker for 2 check-ins.
- User's confidence drops by 2 or more points.
- User requests review of promotion packet, self-review, or manager script.
- AI detects legal, harassment, discrimination, or crisis-adjacent issue.

Escalation products:

- $99 async review.
- $199 strategy review.
- $299 diagnostic plus human review.
- $499 30-day sprint.

## MVP Launch Plan

Week 1:

- Finalize assessments and score formulas.
- Publish landing pages.
- Create payment links.
- Create report template.

Week 2:

- Recruit first 10 beta users.
- Run assessments.
- Deliver scorecards manually or semi-automated.
- Interview users.

Week 3:

- Run weekly check-ins.
- Refine recommendations.
- Test $99 and $299 price points.
- Identify strongest segment.

Week 4:

- Package first case studies.
- Sell next 15 beta slots.
- Decide whether to build lightweight authenticated dashboard.

## Founder Operating Model

Daily:

- Review new assessments.
- Deliver or QA score reports.
- Record user language and objections.
- Capture new insights into knowledge base.

Weekly:

- Review beta metrics.
- Interview 3-5 users.
- Update assessment questions.
- Improve recommendation logic.
- Publish one insight-driven LinkedIn post.

Monthly:

- Review revenue and retention.
- Identify best segment.
- Decide which workflow to automate next.

Revenue milestones:

- First paying customer: sell one $99 diagnostic.
- First 10 customers: direct outreach and founder-led delivery.
- First 25 customers: repeatable beta offer and referral loop.
- First $1,000 MRR: convert diagnostics to monthly check-in plan.
- First $5,000 MRR: add 30-day sprints and human review.
- First $10,000 MRR: scale best-performing diagnostic with paid acquisition or partnerships.
