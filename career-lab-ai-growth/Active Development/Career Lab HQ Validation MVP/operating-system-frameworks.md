# Career Operating System Frameworks

## Career Blueprint

Purpose: convert assessment results into an operating plan.

### Required Fields

Current State:

- Current role
- Current responsibilities
- Current compensation range
- Current satisfaction level
- Current blockers
- Recent accomplishments

Desired State:

- Desired role or responsibility level
- Desired salary
- Desired industry or function
- Desired work environment
- Desired leadership scope

Career Goal:

- Primary 90-day outcome
- 12-month direction
- Why this goal matters
- What success looks like

Target Role:

- Title or role family
- Required capabilities
- Expected outcomes
- Level expectations

Skill Gaps:

- Technical or functional gaps
- Leadership gaps
- Communication gaps
- Influence gaps
- Interview or positioning gaps

Milestones:

- 30-day milestone
- 60-day milestone
- 90-day milestone

Action Plan:

- Weekly actions
- Owner
- Due date
- Expected outcome
- Evidence to capture

Weekly Priorities:

- One high-impact action
- One relationship or visibility action
- One skill or preparation action

Risks:

- Manager misalignment
- Weak evidence
- Low confidence
- Lack of time
- Unclear target
- Workplace politics

Dependencies:

- Manager feedback
- Stakeholder input
- Resume or LinkedIn update
- Interview deadline
- Promotion cycle timing

Success Metrics:

- Score improvement
- Completed actions
- Evidence captured
- Conversations completed
- Interviews scheduled
- Promotion packet readiness

## Weekly Career Check-In

Purpose: maintain progress and update recommendations.

### Check-In Questions

1. Which actions did you complete this week?
2. What did you not complete?
3. What blocked you?
4. What changed at work?
5. What career evidence did you capture?
6. What conversation did you have or avoid?
7. How confident do you feel this week? 1-5.
8. How much momentum do you feel? 1-5.
9. What is the most important outcome next week?

### Score Updates

Update Action Consistency based on completion rate:

- 0% complete: decrease by 10.
- 1-49% complete: decrease by 5.
- 50-79% complete: no change.
- 80-100% complete: increase by 5.

Update Momentum:

`momentum = average(action_completion, confidence, blocker_resolution, evidence_added)`

### AI Follow-Up Recommendations

The AI advisor should:

- Identify the smallest useful next action.
- Reduce plan size if the user is stuck.
- Challenge vague answers.
- Add completed wins to the evidence vault.
- Recommend escalation if the user has a high-stakes event or repeated blocker.

## Promotion Evidence Vault

Purpose: capture proof before the user needs it.

### Evidence Types

- Accomplishments
- Business impact
- Recognition
- Stakeholder feedback
- Projects
- Leadership examples
- Metrics
- Process improvements
- Cross-functional contributions
- Customer or client outcomes

### Evidence Fields

- Title
- Date
- Context
- Action taken
- Result
- Metric
- Stakeholders involved
- Skills demonstrated
- Leadership signal
- Strategic relevance
- Source or attachment

### Promotion Packet Output

Eventually generate:

- Executive summary
- Role target
- Evidence by competency
- Business impact highlights
- Stakeholder quotes
- Growth since last review
- Proposed next-level scope
- Manager conversation agenda

## Manager Conversation Preparation

### One-On-One Workflow

Inputs:

- Meeting goal
- Current issue
- Desired outcome
- Manager style
- Risks

Output:

- 3 talking points
- 2 questions
- 1 clear ask
- Follow-up note

### Promotion Conversation Workflow

Inputs:

- Target role or level
- Promotion evidence
- Manager feedback
- Timing
- Decision process

Output:

- Promotion narrative
- Evidence summary
- Conversation script
- Questions to ask
- Follow-up plan

### Performance Review Workflow

Inputs:

- Review cycle timing
- Accomplishments
- Feedback received
- Development areas
- Desired outcome

Output:

- Self-review draft
- Impact summary
- Growth narrative
- Development plan

### Development Discussion Workflow

Inputs:

- Skill gaps
- Career goal
- Current projects
- Support needed

Output:

- Development agenda
- Ask for support
- Milestone plan
- Accountability follow-up

### Difficult Conversation Workflow

Inputs:

- Situation
- Stakeholders
- Facts
- Emotions
- Desired outcome
- Risks

Output:

- Issue framing
- Boundary language
- Clarifying questions
- Escalation recommendation
- Follow-up summary
