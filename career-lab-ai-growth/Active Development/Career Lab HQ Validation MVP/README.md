# Career Lab HQ Validation MVP

Active objective: validate whether users will pay for the smallest Career Operating System before building the full platform.

North Star Metric: Weekly Completed Career Actions Per Active Member.

Phase 1 product promise:

Career Lab HQ shows where you stand, what is blocking you, and what to do next.

## Build Now

Required MVP:

- Career Health Assessment
- Promotion Readiness Assessment
- Leadership Readiness Assessment
- New Manager Readiness Assessment
- Career Health Score
- Promotion Readiness Score
- Leadership Readiness Score
- New Manager Readiness Score
- Career Blueprint
- Weekly Career Check-In
- Promotion Evidence Vault
- Manager Conversation Prep
- Beta offer landing pages
- Beta validation plan
- Human escalation model

## Do Not Build Yet

- Community
- Courses
- Certifications
- Enterprise features
- Complex admin
- Advanced resource library
- Corporate programs
- Coaching marketplace

Offer 3: 30-Day Career Operating Sprint

- Price: $499

## Success Criteria

Proceed if:

- 10-25 beta users complete assessment.
- At least 40% of qualified users pay.
- At least 70% say the scorecard showed useful blind spots.
- At least 60% complete first weekly check-in.
- At least 30% request human review or continuation.
- Users describe value as structure, score, plan, and accountability, not chatbot access.

Stop or reposition if:

- Users compare it directly to ChatGPT and do not see extra value.
- Users treat it as a one-time PDF only.
- Weekly check-ins are ignored.
- Most demand is only resume editing.
